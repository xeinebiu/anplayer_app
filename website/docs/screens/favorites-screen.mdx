# Favorites Screen

AN Player allows you to organize your favorite content on a dedicated **Favorites Screen** for quick and easy access.

## Adding Content to Favorites

<Column>
    Adding your favorite content is simple. Just follow these steps:

    1. Tap the **horizontal "More" icon** (⋯) on the content you want to add.
    2. Select the option **"Add to Favorites"**.

    <Row>
        <Image src="/img/favorite_screen_1.png" alt="Discover Screen"/>
        <Image src="/img/favorite_screen_2.png" alt="Discover Screen"/>
    </Row>

    Your favorited content will then be saved and can be found on the **Favorites Screen**. To access it, simply tap
    **"Favorites"** in the bottom navigation menu.

    <Image src="/img/favorite_screen_3.png" alt="Discover Screen"/>

</Column>

## Removing Content from Favorites

<Column>
    To remove an item from your favorites:

    1. Tap the **horizontal "More" icon** (⋯) on the item you want to remove.
    2. Select the option **"Remove from Favorites"**.

    The item will no longer appear in your Favorites Screen.

    <Image src="/img/favorite_screen_4.png"/>

</Column>
